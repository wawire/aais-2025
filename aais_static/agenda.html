Africa Aviation Innovation Summit 2025 Agenda

Theme: Investing in Africa’s Aviation Future: Unlocking Opportunities for Growth and Transformation through Innovation

Mombasa, Kenya • 06th – 07th November 2025

Welcome to AAIS 2025

4th Edition

Africa Aviation Innovation Summit

Event Information

Dates:November 6-7, 2025
Setup:November 5, 2025
Venue:Diani Reef Beach Resort, Diani, Kenya
Organizer:Kenya Airways PLC
Contact:aais@kenya-airways.com

Summit Objectives

• Drive investment opportunities in aviation infrastructure and innovation

• Highlight innovation's role in enhancing efficiency and operational excellence

• Showcase funding models and business strategies for industry resilience

• Foster regulatory dialogue for an enabling investment environment

Why Diani?

Diani, the jewel of Kenya's South Coast, provides an ideal setting for networking against the backdrop of pristine beaches, luxury resorts, and rich cultural experiences.

• Strategic coastal destination accessible via Ukunda Airstrip

• Key tourism and economic hub on Kenya's South Coast

• Excellence in tourism and hospitality

• Supportive business environment for aviation and investment

Target Audience

• Airline executives
• Airport operators
• Government representatives
• Investors & financiers
• Tourism leaders
• Technology providers
• Trade professionals
• Sustainability experts
